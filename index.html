Milestone

Birthdays & Anniversaries

Milestone makes it easy to keep track of the birthdays and anniversaries that matter most to you. Add a date and instantly see ages, countdowns, zodiac signs, birthstones, and more.

  • Birthday tracking with automatic age calculation
  • Anniversary tracking with countdowns
  • Zodiac sign, birthstone, and generation info
  • School grade calculation for kids and students
  • Search and sort your lists
  • Notification reminders for upcoming dates
  • All data stored locally on your device
